**Stranger Wine Co.** is a family-owned micro-winery in Buchanan, Michigan, rooted in the Lake Michigan Shore AVA. Founded in 2021 by husband-and-wife team Maxx Eichberg and Sidney Finan, the winery sprang from their shared [passion for wine and agriculture](https://www.strangerwineco.com/story#:~:text=Absurdists%20at%20heart%2C%20Stranger%20Wine,Stranger%20Wine%20Company%20in%202021) and a desire to "find meaning in an otherwise meaningless world" through winemaking. They purchased the historic [15-acre Avonlea Estate vineyard](https://creamwine.com/brand.php?id=1695#:~:text=Husband%20and%20wife%20team%2C%20Maxx,are%20extremely%20diverse%20and%20change) in 2021 after [working a harvest in southwest Michigan](https://vinepair.com/articles/climate-migrants-great-lakes-wineries/#:~:text=That%20didn%E2%80%99t%20stop%20husband,to%20see%20which%20adapt%20best) changed their original plan to launch a winery in Oregon.

### History

The [Avonlea Estate](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=Our%20estate%20vineyard%2C%20Avonlea%2C%20is,health%20through%20our%20farming%20practices) includes some of Michigan’s oldest vinifera vines, dating back to the late 1980s. The winery earned [MAEAP environmental verification](https://creamwine.com/brand.php?id=1695#:~:text=In%202021%20Maxx%20and%20Sidney,a%20more%20sustainable%20agricultural%20future) in its first year, reflecting their commitment to responsible agriculture. Their 2021 Pinot Noir received 91 points and early acclaim, affirming their belief in southwest Michigan’s terroir.

### Viticulture

Avonlea is located in Buchanan, MI, and sits within the [Lake Michigan Shore AVA](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=Climate%3A%20Avonlea%20resides%20in%20the,untapped%20potential%20and%20exciting%20future). It features 15 acres of mature vinifera vines including [Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Petit Verdot, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, and Riesling](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=Our%20estate%20vineyard%2C%20Avonlea%2C%20is,health%20through%20our%20farming%20practices). The site’s [diverse glacial soils](https://creamwine.com/brand.php?id=1695#:~:text=Husband%20and%20wife%20team%2C%20Maxx,grown%20on%20this%20soil%20produce) include [iron-rich clay and sand](https://creamwine.com/brand.php?id=1695#:~:text=iron%20rich%20clay%20and%20sand,of%20great%20tension%20and%20length), [gravel loam with chert and granite](https://creamwine.com/brand.php?id=1695#:~:text=wines%20of%20great%20tension%20and,length), and a [band of blue clay](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=The%20middle%20hill%20on%20Avonlea,10%20years%20in%20bottle) ideal for Riesling.

The vineyard is farmed [organically](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=site%20is%20home%20to%20vinifera,health%20through%20our%20farming%20practices) and regeneratively. The team avoids herbicides and synthetic pesticides, instead using [biological fungicides](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=Over%20the%20past%20several%20years%2C,the%20application%20of%20harsh%20fungicides), [hand labor for all major vineyard tasks](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=All%20of%20our%20labor%20,output%20and%20avoid%20soil%20compaction), and even [grazing sheep](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=minimize%20our%20CO2%20output%20and,avoid%20soil%20compaction) to mow and fertilize. Wild plantings and [pollinator habitats](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=With%20the%20goal%20of%20biodiversity,grow%20as%20they%20otherwise%20would) are maintained between rows.

They also farm other Berrien County sites including [Old Shore Vineyard](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=During%20the%202024%20growing%20season,regenerative%20practices%20at%20this%20vineyard) and source from partners like [Tom’s Vineyard and Outcross Vineyard](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=Here%20are%20some%20of%20the,Outcross%20Vineyard%E2%80%93%20Benton%20Harbor%2C%20MI). All grapes are [100% Michigan-grown](https://www.strangerwineco.com/viticulture#:~:text=Here%20are%20some%20of%20the,Outcross%20Vineyard%E2%80%93%20Benton%20Harbor%2C%20MI).

### Winemaking

In the cellar, Stranger follows a [low-intervention philosophy](https://www.strangerwineco.com/shop#:~:text=of%20our%20commitment%20to%20highlighting,lees%20to%20reduce%20sulfur%20use). Fermentations use native yeast, and wines are [unfined and unfiltered](https://www.strangerwineco.com/shop#:~:text=produce%20single%20varietal%2C%20single%20vineyard,at%20bottling). Sulfur is used sparingly, often only at bottling and under 30 ppm. Most wines are [aged on lees](https://www.strangerwineco.com/shop#:~:text=produce%20single%20varietal%2C%20single%20vineyard,at%20bottling) in neutral vessels, helping reduce intervention and build texture.

They work with a variety of fermentation styles, such as [carbonic maceration](https://michigan.guides.winefolly.com/wineries/stranger-wine-company/wines/viognier-stranger-wine-company/#:~:text=Stranger%20Wine%20Company%20Cabernet%20Franc,with%20spice%20and%20lively%20acidity) for Pinot Noir and brief [skin contact for Viognier](https://michigan.guides.winefolly.com/wineries/stranger-wine-company/wines/viognier-stranger-wine-company/#:~:text=Skin%20contact%20for%205%20hours,No%20SO2). Whites like Grüner Veltliner and Chardonnay are fermented in neutral oak with long lees aging. All wines are 100% vinifera and typically [single-varietal, single-vineyard bottlings](https://www.strangerwineco.com/shop#:~:text=Stranger%20Wine%20Company%20is%20dedicated,at%20bottling).
